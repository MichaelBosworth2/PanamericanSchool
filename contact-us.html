The best way to get a feel for Escuela Panamericana is to visit campus. Appointments are scheduled while classes are in session to afford families the opportunity to see the school in action and to interact with our community. Tell us if you have a particular area of interest that you would like to explore while on campus and we will schedule a meeting with a faculty member or a coach.

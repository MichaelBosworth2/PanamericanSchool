Mission & Vision

“Create a vision for the life you really want and then work relentlessly towards making it a reality.”

Our MISSION is to provide an international bicultural, bilingual program of studies which satisfies the needs of our students-both now and in the future. Students will receive knowledge, insight, character and the encouragement necessary for their Christian, moral, social and civic development, as well as a love of learning. Our resources provide students with the educational and technological background necessary to succeed in any selected institution of higher learning or career choice.

Our VISION is to produce graduates who will be capable of facing new challenges by achieving their full potential; academically, socially and emotionally, along with a solid and balanced system of Christian values that will guide them throughout their lives. The staff of Escuela Panamericana will work with each individual student to help him/her develop his/her potential as a productive, balanced, and successful adult.

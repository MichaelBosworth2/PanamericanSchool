ACCREDITATIONS & MEMBERSHIPS

Learn more about our Accreditations and Memberships

Escuela Panamerica is fully accredited by: The Southern Association of Colleges and Schools (SACS/AdvancED) in the United States of America.

In El Salvador, by Ministerio de Educacion (MINED).

Our School is also a member of:

The National Honor Society (NHS): As an NHS member, students can get ready for whatever comes their way after high school. It helps them to prepare for every college journey and career. Offers students exclusive access to robust tools and resources.

Association of American Schools in Central America (AASCA): Supports and facilitates academic, artistic, athletic and cultural interaction among its member schools.

International School to School Experience (ISSE): A unique international program for schools that provides for the exchange of thoughts and ideas through face-to-face contact with children from other countries around the world.

School's Beliefs

"A house built on granite and strong foundations, not even the onslaught of pouring rain, gushing torrents and strong winds will be able to pull down."

Escuela Panamericana is preparing students to achieve success now and in their future. We believe every student has the potential to learn and grow, and should have the opportunity to develop their talents and personalities in a safe environment.

Students should always be taught to respect their Creator, family, teachers, and peers. All this in an atmosphere of work, cooperation, respect, acceptance and friendship.
